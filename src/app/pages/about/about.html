About

Justin Hebenstreit

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer and automotive photographer with a deep passion for exotic, luxury vehicles, and the art of capturing them at their best.

My journey into automotive photography began long before I picked up a camera professionally. A lifelong fascination with performance vehicles eventually led me to bring a Canon DSLR to car shows, dealership events, and ultimately a once-in-a-lifetime trip to Italy, where I had the privilege of photographing inside the Lamborghini Museum on their factory grounds in Sant'Agata Bolognese, as well as visiting the Ferrari Museum.

What drives my work is patience and perspective. Whether navigating the uncontrolled lighting of a museum interior, the midday sun at an outdoor car show, or the polished floors of a dealership showroom. I look for the angle and the moment that does the machine justice. Every car has a story. My job is to tell it visually.

I shoot primarily with Canon DSLR equipment and bring the same attention to detail to post-processing as I do to the shoot itself, using Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop to refine each image while keeping it authentic.

Beyond photography, I am an active Full-Stack Web Developer working with technologies including React, TypeScript, Node.js, Angular, Firebase, and more. This portfolio itself was built with Angular 19.
